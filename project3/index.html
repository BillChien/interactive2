THE GREATEST
BEST SONGS OF THE 2000S


The music of the Aughts was all over the map in the very best way, with file sharing and randomly produced personal playlist encouraging eclecticism and experimentation in both artists and listeners. Originated from Rolling Stone’s list of the decade’s 100 best songs, these 10 greatest songs of 2000s so far was compiled by a group of over 100 artists, critics and industry insiders. The most exciting thing about this selection of tunes is that despite all the different styles and voices in the mix, it all sounds totally natural together. In fact, you might already have a playlist that looks just like it.
